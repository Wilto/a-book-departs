* Canada: Chapters / Indigo 
* Sweden: Adlibris
* UK: Foyles, Waterstones, Blackwell’s
* Germany: Thalia.de
* Australia and New Zealand: Fishpond
* Ebook: Apple, Barnes & Noble Nook, Kobo
